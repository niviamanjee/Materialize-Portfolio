About Me

Career and Education

Hello Everyone! Welcome to my website. My name is Nivedita Amanjee, and I go by Nivi. My background reflects a love for learning, a drive to innovate solutions and implement knowledge gained from university in the workforce, and the ambition to execute efficient, reliable code for the future company I collaborate with. My past experince includes creating and executing therapy plans for my clients as a behavioral therapist at Center for Autism and Related Disorders, using Customer Relation Management software and in-person interaction with business owners to increase local business participation at Blue Line Arts Gallery as a business development intern, and demonstrating data structures, algorithms, graphics and programming concepts as a computer science instructor at Juni Learning. With these various endeavors, I bring a diverse set of skills incorporating psychology, business, and technology.

I am currently a computer science major undergraduate at the University of California, Davis with a full stack web development bootcamp certificate from the UC Davis Continuing and Professional Program. My relevant coursework includes data structures and algorithms, computational theory, computer architecture, and operating systems. I am currently pursuing the topics of bioinformatics, programming languages, and artificial intelligence. In the future, I strive to complete coursework and certification in machine learning and cloud computing.

Hobbies and Interests

In my free time, I enjoy painting, singing, exercising, and inventing new business and application ideas. I love watching comedy shows and spending time with my friends. I focus on constantly improving myself by working towards various goals I set for myself. For example, I recently completed a half marathon and strive to compete at a body building competition someday.
