About Me

Career and Education

Hello Everyone! Welcome to my website. My name is Nivedita Amanjee, and I go by Nivi. My background reflects a love for learning and a drive to innovate and develop better interactions between my clients and the world around them. From creating and executing therapy plans for my clients as a behavioral therapist at Center for Autism and Related Disorders, to using Customer Relation Management software and in-person interaction with business owners to increase local business participation at Blue Line Arts Gallery, where I worked as a business development intern, I hope to incorporate the interconnected culture of psychology and business into my passion of web development and technology. I am currently a computer science undergraduate at American River College and developer in a full-stack web development certificate program at University of California, Davis. In the future, I hope to provide outstanding user experience for the websites I create.

Hobbies and Interests

In my free time, I enjoy writing, painting, singing, and inventing new business and application ideas. I love watching comedy shows and spending time with my friends. I try to keep myself active and in shape by running and hitting the gym. Let's hope life as a programmer doesn't make me age three times as fast!
